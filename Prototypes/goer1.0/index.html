Journal

Updated "when I feel like it", which these days translates to about one entry a week. There's very little Big Serious Personal Life stuff here -- it's really just a writing exercise, a scrapbook of mini-essays and snippets that I hope my close friends and family will find entertaining. Here's hoping you do too.

Latest Entry:
February 26, Sickness: M'ris suspects that my poker buddies were just afraid to take my germy money. Shows how much she knows -- I could soak my wallet in untreated Ebola-infected monkey blood and my poker buddies would still be falling over themselves to take my money.
